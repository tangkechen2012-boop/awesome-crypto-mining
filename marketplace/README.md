# ♻️ Used Miner Marketplace

This is a **privacy-preserving marketplace** for buying and selling used cryptocurrency mining hardware. Seller contact information is **never published** — all transactions are facilitated through our AI middleman, protecting both buyers and sellers throughout the process.

---

## How to Buy

Ready to find a deal? Reach out to our AI middleman through any of these channels:

1. 💬 **Telegram:** [@MiningDealBot](https://t.me/MiningDealBot)
2. 🌐 **Web form:** [bt-miners.com/marketplace](https://bt-miners.com/marketplace)
3. 📧 **Email:** deals@bt-miners.com

### What to Have Ready When Inquiring

To speed up the process, have the following information on hand:

- **Listing ID** — found in the listings table (e.g., `UM-001`)
- **Quantity** — how many units you need
- **Your location** — for shipping logistics and cost estimates
- **Your budget** — helps us find alternatives if the listed unit is sold
- **Timeline** — when you need the miners delivered

---

## How to Sell

Email **deals@bt-miners.com** with the following details:

- **Miner model** (e.g., Antminer S19j Pro 104TH/s)
- **Quantity** available
- **Condition:** working / needs repair / for parts
- **Asking price per unit** (USD)
- **Photos** (optional but highly recommended — increases buyer confidence)

Our team will review your submission and **post your listing within 24 hours**.

---

## Privacy Policy

- **Seller contact info stays with the bt-miners team** — it is never published in listings or shared unsolicited.
- **Buyers are connected to sellers only after both parties express intent** — no cold exposure of either party's details.
- All communication goes through our middleman until both buyer and seller are ready to proceed.
