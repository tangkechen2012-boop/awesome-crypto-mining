## Overview

Zcash is a privacy-focused cryptocurrency that uses zero-knowledge proofs (zk-SNARKs) to allow fully shielded transactions. It was the first practical implementation of zk-SNARKs in a cryptocurrency. Zcash uses the Equihash proof-of-work algorithm, which was originally designed to be ASIC-resistant but has since seen dedicated ASIC miners dominate the network. ZEC has a fixed supply of 21 million coins and a similar halving schedule to Bitcoin.

## Is ZEC Worth Mining Right Now?

Zcash mining margins are modest at current prices. The Antminer Z15 Pro remains the most efficient option, and at $0.06/kWh it can generate a small positive return. However, ZEC's price has been in a prolonged downtrend, making it a harder sell compared to BTC or KAS. Miners interested in ZEC often do so for ideological reasons (privacy coins) or because they have existing Equihash hardware. Worth monitoring for price rebounds but not a top pick for pure profitability seekers.
