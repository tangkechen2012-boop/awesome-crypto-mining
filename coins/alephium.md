# Alephium (ALPH) Mining Guide

> **Algorithm:** Blake3 | **Current Price:** ~$1.10 | **[Price History](price-history/alph.csv)**

## Overview

Alephium is a sharded, proof-of-work blockchain that combines UTXO and smart contract capabilities through its novel BlockFlow sharding algorithm. It uses the Blake3 hashing algorithm — one of the fastest cryptographic hash functions available — enabling highly energy-efficient mining compared to SHA-256 or Ethash. ALPH is an emerging coin with a growing developer ecosystem and a design philosophy that emphasizes both scalability and security without sacrificing decentralization.

## Is ALPH Worth Mining Right Now?

Alephium is one of the more compelling emerging PoW coins for 2025–2026. Its Blake3 algorithm allows for very energy-efficient ASICs, and the Goldshell AL-BOX series offers a low barrier to entry for home miners. At $1.10/ALPH and $0.06/kWh, the AL-BOX generates a small but positive daily return. ALPH's network is still relatively young, meaning early miners benefit from lower competition than established coins. The main risk is price volatility — as an emerging asset, ALPH can swing significantly. Diversifying with a mix of ALPH and more established coins is a common strategy among newer miners.
