> **Algorithm:** kHeavyHash | **Current Price:** ~$0.12 | **[Price History](price-history/kas.csv)**

## Overview

Kaspa is a proof-of-work cryptocurrency built on a novel BlockDAG (Directed Acyclic Graph) architecture that allows near-instant block confirmations at roughly one block per second. Unlike traditional blockchains, Kaspa doesn't discard parallel blocks — it integrates them, enabling high throughput without sacrificing decentralization. KAS has gained a strong following among miners due to its ASIC-friendly kHeavyHash algorithm and active development team.

## Is KAS Worth Mining Right Now?

Kaspa's deflationary emission schedule means block rewards decrease about 1% per month, gradually reducing miner income unless KAS price appreciates. At current prices around $0.12 and with efficient hardware like the KS5 Pro, miners at $0.06/kWh can still operate profitably. KAS is considered one of the more speculative but promising PoW assets given its technical innovation. Miners should watch the network hashrate growth closely, as rapid ASIC adoption can quickly compress margins.
