# Litecoin (LTC) Mining Guide

> **Algorithm:** Scrypt | **Current Price:** ~$85 | **[Price History](price-history/ltc.csv)**

## Overview

Litecoin is one of the oldest and most established cryptocurrencies, often described as the "silver to Bitcoin's gold." It uses the Scrypt hashing algorithm and produces blocks four times faster than Bitcoin. A key advantage for miners: Litecoin can be **merge-mined with Dogecoin** at no extra power cost, effectively doubling the revenue stream from a single miner. This makes LTC/DOGE dual-mining rigs among the most practical in the industry.

## Is LTC Worth Mining Right Now?

When factoring in Dogecoin merge-mining rewards, LTC miners earn significantly more than LTC block rewards alone suggest. The Antminer L9 and Elphapex DG1 are currently the most efficient Scrypt ASICs available, and at $0.06–$0.08/kWh both remain profitable. LTC mining is a lower-risk entry point compared to BTC because the hardware is cheaper and the dual-mining bonus provides a cushion against price dips in either coin. The next LTC halving is expected in 2027, which will reduce block rewards to 3.125 LTC.
